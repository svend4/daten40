# IFOS 80101–80500: Financial Planning & Budgeting (FP&A) OS

## Purpose
Provide unified planning, budgeting, forecasting, and plan-vs-actual capabilities across entities, scenarios, and time horizons.

## Core Capabilities
- Annual & rolling budgets
- Forecasts (rolling, driver-based)
- Scenarios (base / best / worst / custom)
- Plan-vs-Actual variance analysis
- Driver models & assumptions
- Workforce planning (headcount, comp)
- Capital planning (CapEx, depreciation)
- Approval workflows & versioning

## Key Objects
- Scenario
- Budget
- Forecast
- Assumption / Driver
- Model (formula graph)
- Approval
- Variance

## Time Dimensions
- Fiscal year, period, week
- Rolling windows (e.g., T+12)
- Locking & reforecast cadence

## Integrations
- Accounting (actuals ingestion)
- HR (headcount, comp)
- Procurement (spend)
- Revenue (pipeline, bookings)

## Governance
- Scenario permissions
- Approval gates
- Audit trail & snapshots

## Outputs
- P&L, Cash Flow, Balance Sheet (planned)
- Variance reports
- Waterfalls
